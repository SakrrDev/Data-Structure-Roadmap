## 10. Which of the following represents the **best average-case complexity** for comparison-based sorting algorithms?
A. O(n²)  
B. O(log n)  
C. O(n log n)  
D. O(n)  

---

##  Answers

1. **D. O(2ⁿ)**  
2. **C. O(n)**  
3. **C. It becomes four times larger**  
4. **B. O(log n)**  
5. **B. O(n²)**  
6. **C. O(n)**  
7. **C. O(n²)**  
8. **D. O(2ⁿ)**  
9. **B. Runtime is constant regardless of input size**  
10. **C. O(n log n)**
